About Me

Discover My Journey

I am a passionate Computer Science and Engineering graduate, with strong interest in the application of Artificial Intelligence, Machine and Deep Learning. During my BSc, I covered a vast number of research and development fields - natural language processing and explainable AI, computer vision, and IoT systems. Some of my academic projects are Bangla News Classification using LSTM Networks, Sylheti-to-Modern Bangali Translation using Seq2seq Models and Fake news Detection in Malayalam using Fine-tuned MuRIL BERT which were published in world-renowned IEEE and EACL conferences.

In addition to AI, I have designed a Smart Inventory Management System which is based on IoT and monitors in real time on ThingSpeak, and has implemented sophisticated image preprocessing methods to analyze the plant leaves. I am interested in innovation in the reinforcement learning, contextual question answering (RAG-based systems), and personalized learning systems. My motivation is the desire to bring together technical excellence and research acuity to create smart, data-driven systems that can bring technology closer to the people and more effective.
